Behavior Notes

  • NSPanel with .floating level — always on top, no Dock icon
  • Appears instantly when processing starts, positioned at screen top-center
  • Shows current file being processed and overall progress (X of N)
  • Shows space saved — running total of bytes saved during this batch
  • Completed state: green checkmark, summary with skip details, auto-dismiss after 2 seconds
  • Skipped files: shown in amber/yellow at the bottom of the completed state
  • Completion sound: plays when panel transitions to "Done!" state
  • Window size: ~360x160pt, non-resizable, no title bar buttons
